Introduction

Hi! My name is Hayden Brophy. I am a professional aerospace engineer currently working at SpaceX. My engineering career began at the USC Rocket Propulsion Lab, where I helped design and build the world's highest-flying and fastest amateur rocket, Aftershock II. Outside of engineering, I'm an avid hiker and overall adventure enjoyer, including but not limited to: mountaineering, rock climbing, backpacking, and sailing. I maintain this site as a personal engineering log for experimental projects and design documentation.
