About the Author

H. Brophy Engineering Log

Biography

H. Brophy is an amateur rocketry enthusiast and engineering hobbyist with a passion for experimental propulsion systems. This site serves as a technical log for ongoing projects, test data, and design documentation.

Background

  • B.S. Mechanical Engineering
  • Member, National Association of Rocketry (NAR)
  • Tripoli Rocketry Association Member
  • Level 2 High Power Rocketry Certification

Areas of Interest

  • Solid propellant motor design
  • Static testing methodologies
  • Thrust curve analysis
  • Recovery system development
  • Composite airframe construction
  • Flight data acquisition systems

Current Projects

Currently working on a series of experimental solid fuel motors ranging from H-class to M-class impulse. All testing is conducted in accordance with NAR and Tripoli safety codes at certified launch sites.
